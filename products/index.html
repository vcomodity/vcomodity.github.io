Since our formation in 1979, we have steadily expanded the volume handled both for our parent companies and for other domestic sugar producers. Midwest Agri markets beet pulp pellets, beet pulp shreds, beet molasses and raffinate (desugared beet molasses) with a substantial share in all the markets served.

These co-products are used in pet food manufacturing, as an enhancer in milk production, bakers yeast manufacturing and a host of other feed related applications.

Midwest Agri also markets cane molasses produced by the Hawaiian sugar industry, handling logistics and distribution for this product to the U.S. West Coast.
