Quality Control

Vision Commodities’s Quality department focuses on two approaches: quality control and quality assurance. Whereas quality control focuses on monitoring and maintaining Vision Commodities’s high level of quality in the ongoing delivery of Vision Commodities’s products and services, quality assurance focuses on ensuring that Vision Commodities’s operations are always on the leading edge of food safety principles as well as regulatory requirements.

To attain this goal, Vision Commodities boasts the following programs:

  • Organic Manufacturing
  • Food Safety, Quality and Food Defense
  • Good Manufacturing Practices
  • Kosher Pareve
  • GFSI (planned 2016)

Additionally, Vision Commodities works on a positive release system where product lots are only released after they are thoroughly checked to ensure they conform to the highest quality standards.
