Directions

From the North

  1. Take I-95 South into Delaware
  2. Take exit 1a (towards RT 896 South / Middletown)
  3. Take a right on the 4th traffic light into Pencader Corporate Plaza (onto Corporate Blvd.)
  4. Take a left at the first stop sign (onto Pencader Drive)
  5. Follow the road around the natural right turn. Vision Commodities is located on your left side, and is the second to last building in the complex.

From the South

  1. Take I-95 North in Maryland
  2. Take exit 109 for Maryland 279 N toward Newark, DE.
  3. Continue on the ramp and merge onto MD-279 E / Elkton Road. Continue to follow Elkton Road entering Delaware
  4. Turn right onto Otts Chapel Road.
  5. Continue onto Pleasant Valley Road
  6. Turn Left onto Pencader Drive. Vision Commodities is located on your right side, and is the second building in the complex.
