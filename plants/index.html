Our parent companies — American Crystal Sugar Company, Minn-Dak Farmers Cooperative, Southern Minnesota Beet Sugar Cooperative and Michigan Sugar Company — operate fifteen beet sugar plants strategically located throughout the United States.

Close proximity to the Great Lakes, the Mississippi River system and the westbound rail network allows efficient distribution of product to export markets. Good rail connections also provide easy access to domestic customers who can receive shipment by railcar.

A fleet of railcars offers delivery of liquid products throughout the United States, while truck delivery of both beet pulp and liquid products is available for local customers.
